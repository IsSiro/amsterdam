How it works

1. What is it

Let's talk straight. If you want to see old buildings and all this "classic" stuff, you have a huge amount of tours in most of Europe cities. We decide to start showing you Amsterdam in a way, like we show it to our friends, not clients. Yes, we have "sightseeing", but there is no castles, only Heineken factory â€” a huge beer castle. And if you ask almost anywhere in the world what people associate Amtserdam with, you will hear tulips, red light district and drugs. We want to prove that Amsterdam has a lot more colors and shades.

2. What can I visit

We have five programs for you with different activities: sightseeing, relax, bars, adults only and night trips. They are availale everyday but clearly on schedule. Every program have 3 different guides, so you can choose any program with three different start times.

3. What about groups?

Each group shoud have 7-10 people to in order for the program to take place. But you can order individual program to visit everything in small group or short ones, if you don't really interested in whole program.

4. What about prices?

On the program page you will find three prices: standard, short, individual. This is the price per person (and in an individual program, you can be only one person in a group). We don't offer discounts for those who take several programs, as we try to make sure that you almost do not notice how one program has flowed into another and you are already being guided by a new guide. You can even spend the whole day with our guides, as our five programs fit in exactly 24 hours. In this case, there are no discounts, but you will get a really comprehensive experience from visiting Amsterdam.

F. A. Q.

Q: I'm late, what should I do?

A: Every day we have three guides for each program within an hour of each other, so you can join the next group. If this was the last start of the program today, you can call the guide and try to catch up with them. The guide's telephone number is always sent in a text message along with the program confirmation.

Q: I want to take my child with me, is there an age limit?

A: Daytime programs have no restrictions if the child is accompanied by an adult. But the "night trips" and "adults only" programs have restrictions. In "night trips" you can take a child at least 14 years old, but "adults only" is strictly from 18 years and older. If you have no place to leave your child for this time, and then you go on "night trips", you can leave your child absolutely free of charge in our office, where there is a small library and our Internet cafe.

Q :I am over 18 years old, do I need to prove it?

A: We don't need to prove anything, we hope for your honesty. But be prepared to confirm your age with a document, if this is required by employees of bars and night clubs. If you are refused entry because you don't have your documents with you, the company is not responsible.

Q :What if I want to revisit your program?

A: We promice you, that every program could be different everyday with the same guide, because they're tired to repeat program word by word everyday. So there are so much fun and a lot of unpredictability.
